Aim of the Website

This website aims to list down every Ophthalmologist within Cebu in a precise manner so the
users of this website can just search the area near them and see the avaible Ophthalmologist.

Mission

To utilize the advantages of our developing technology, we offer our customers to have
the easiest and most efficient way to view the list of Ophthalmologists where they can
perceive its location, contact number, and email address with ease.

Vission

Optimax Inc. envisions to be a prominent website that would produce quality information,
starting with the list of Ophthalmologist. It also aspires to be a social enterprise that cares
for its customers by providing them with good quality information on eye care and for its
doctors by hiring them initially as contractual workers with an intention to promote them
as regular workers.
